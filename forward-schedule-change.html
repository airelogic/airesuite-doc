📅 Forward Schedule of Change ​

The Forward Schedule of Change (FSC) provides customers with advance visibility of planned AireBlocks releases, maintenance and platform changes. This ensures organisations can plan, review and complete any required local assurance activities ahead of deployment. All AireBlocks releases follow a governed testing and assurance cycle to ensure changes are safe, stable and ready for production use.

Change Types ​

  • Standard – Low-risk, routine, pre-approved change
  • Normal – Planned change requiring review and approval
  • HotFix – Urgent fix for critical issues / System defects / Security-driven urgent changes
